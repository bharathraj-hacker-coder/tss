    About Team Sea Sakthi

    Team Sea Sakthi is the first team to represent India at the Monaco Energy Boat Challenge. Possessing some of the finest minds in KCT, TSS ideates, designs, and fabricates next-gen electric boats. With an eye towards sustainability, and passion for progress, the 15 strong TSS raises to challenges and innovates all time.

    About Garage

    Team Sea Sakthi is one of the team that grew out of KCT Garage. Garage is a student involved Product Development – Research Centre of the automotive sector. Students here design, analyze and fabricate different type of vehicles that include terrain vehicle, supra vehicle, quad bike, dirt bike, go – kart and solar karts. Students of KCT Garage compete in various motor sporting events around the globe.

    About KCT

    Kumaraguru College of Technology has been a spawn point of innovative engineers for nearly four decades. The institution has been a significant contributor to the advancements of knowledge in teaching-learning, research and administration. It has been consistently ranked among the best engineering institutions in India. Situated in a sprawling 156-acre campus in the IT corridor of Coimbatore, KCT has the motto “Character is life” and emphasises the transformation of students into trailblazing individuals.

    Sakthi Group of Companies

    Sakthi Groups, founded in 1931 by Shri. P, Nachimuthu Gounder and expanded upon by Arutchelvar Dr. N. Mahalingam, is a multifaceted organization with branches serving transport, automobile spares, IT, Sugars, food, textiles, finance and education. With a strong and growing market presence, Sakthi Group has been playing a significant role in the social and economic development of Southern India whilst spreading it's presence all over the globe. Sakthi Group has always been keen on societal impact, founding a number of trusts and schools for the benefit of the populous. Sakthi group is the namesake for our team, Sea Sakthi. We are more than over joyed to receive the support and backing of Sakthi Groups and are set to do them proud in Monaco.
