    About KCT

    Kumaraguru College of Technology is a spawn point of innovative engineers for nearly four decades. KCT has been a significant contributor to the advancements of knowledge in teaching-learning, research and administration, and thus been consistently ranked among the best engineering institutions in India. Situated in a sprawling 156-acre campus in the IT corridor of Coimbatore, KCT has the moto “Character is life” and emphasises transformation of students into trailblazing individuals.

    About Team Sea Sakthi

    Team Sea Sakthi is the first team to represent India at the Monaco Energy Boat Challenge. Possessing some of the finest minds in KCT, TSS ideates, designs, and fabricates next-gen electric boats. With an eye towards sustainability, and passion for progress, the 15 strong TSS raises to challenges and innovates all time.

    About Garage

    KCT Garage is a student-based automotive research forum. It is a place for budding engineers to learn, practise and hone. Teams under Garage contest in and bring laurels from automotive events across India. KCT Garage is also keen on sustainability and regularly conducts research on and develops electric vehicles.
