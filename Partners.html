    Industry Partners
    CHIEF TECHNOLOGY PARTNER

    NavAlt is the award-winning manufacturer of efficient and sustainable marine transportation solutions. They’ve initiated groundbreaking achievements and industry leading feats, such as India's first solar powered ferry, named ADITYA. Innovation in Solar-powered electric vessels are the core focus of NavAlt. Team Sea Sakthi has developed an enduring partnership with NavAlt, through which the student team receives technical expertise and technological aid. The propulsion and power systems of the team’s boat has been developed by NavAlt.

    NavAlt
    MANAGEMENT PARTNER

    IndSight growth partners is a consultancy company helping Indian and International companies with their business plans. Based in Delhi and Mumbai, India, they provide services on business strategy, organizational strategy, sales and marketing and mergers support to clients all across the board. Team Sea Sakthi has formed a partnership with IndSight through which the team receives business and strategic planning support.

    IndSight
    MERCHANDISING PARTNER

Arasu and Sethupathi of Master Linen Inc., Karur are the entrepreneurs who have seen impressive growth through their faith in the natural fibre.
