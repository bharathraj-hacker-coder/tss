    SAKTHI
    Team Sea Sakthi A power-packed team of 15 Engineering Students, of various ingenious talents from different departments of Kumaraguru College of Technology, Coimbatore. We strive to be the creative minds defending marine industry from air pollution via technological advances. We are the first Indian team at Monaco Energy Boat Challenge.
    Read More...
    Mission

    Our objective is to Electrify the Sea. We develop sustainable systems in Marine Industry and contribute to saving our planet. To stand as a shining example of Indian innovation and progress at the world stage.

    Vision

    Sustainability at sea; This is our vision. A world where shipping, fishing, and leisure watercraft are all electric. A futuristic fleet for the future and we are at the forefront of this transformation.

    MEBC

    Monaco is the world capital of yachting and Yacht Club de Monaco is the most prestigious yachting society. Organized by such pioneers, Monaco Boat Energy Challenge is one of its kind. With over 20 nations converging to create, innovate, and present the future of marine transport, MEBC unites yachting projects around zero emission propulsion and sustainability.
